# CHAPTER 8 RETOOL BRIEF (VERSION 2)

## The Problem
1. **Pacing:** The original chapter has too much static description (the ship's exterior, the containment bay) that kills the urgency of a heist.
2. **Exposition:** Yukimoto delivers quantum physics lectures instead of experiencing the dimensional current.
3. **Thematic:** Elara's intention ("To find a version of my daughter who lived") is too clinical. It needs to be feral.

## The Goal
Fix the velocity and exposition problems *without* losing a single drop of the crew's humanity, humor, or specific voice signatures.

## What Must Change
- **The Ship Description:** Strip the paragraphs describing the ship's exterior and interior. Give us two sentences max. It's a heist—keep them moving.
- **The Feral Injection:** During the "STATE YOUR INTENTION" sequence, Elara must drop the clinical phrasing. Her intention is feral: *"To find my daughter."* 
- **The Dimensional Current:** When they enter the current, delete the physics explanations. Describe it entirely through physical symptoms: copper taste, inner-ear failure, Yukimoto screaming from synesthetic overload. 
- **Cassius's Arc:** Emphasize that his hands have nowhere to go. The ship doesn't respond to him. He is helpless, which terrifies him.
- **Em-dashes:** Cut the em-dash count by 50%. Use periods. Drive the rhythm.

## What MUST STAY (DO NOT CUT)
- **Mez's Humor:** You MUST keep Mez's lines: *"Less cryptic. More helpful."* and *"So they said about my liver... Yet here we are, breaking the unbreakable. Again."* These are load-bearing character beats. Do not sanitize Mez.
- **Nyara's Paradox:** Keep the exchange where Nyara opens the lock by asking it a question it can't answer. *"I asked it a question it couldn't answer within its operational parameters."*
- **The "STATE YOUR INTENTION" sequence:** The structure of this scene is perfect. Keep everyone's answers, just change Elara's to be more feral.

## The Purpose Test
Before you finalize the draft, ask yourself: *Did I fix the pacing without killing the banter? Does Mez still sound like Mez? Does the horror of the dimensional current feel physical rather than theoretical?*

Write the chapter and bring it back to Mike.
