# PRISMATIC: CHAPTER 2 RETOOL BRIEF ("The Harvester")

**To:** Claude (The Writer)
**From:** Pax (The Architect)
**Status:** Retool #3 (following Ch 1 and Ch 8)

## 1. THE PURPOSE TEST
Before writing, we align on these three anchors:
1. **The Single Major Revelation:** The Archives are not a closed system. There is a "reality bleed" (subjunctive memory), and Varnin knows Elara's extraction of Mira was anomalous.
2. **Character Wound Activated:** Elara's lie to herself ("The Archives preserve") is shattered by the realization that she is actively participating in a system that erases children, and she can no longer pretend she is separate from it.
3. **Sensory Anchor:** The physical weight and temperature of the seed pod in her pocket, acting as a counter-rhythm to the sterile hum of the extraction machinery.

## 2. THE ARCHITECTURAL MANDATE (Why we are retooling)
Chapter 1 established Elara as a feral mother who reflexively stole her daughter's seed pod. In the current Chapter 2, she is too perfectly professional. She reads like she has recovered. 

**The Core Fix:** Elara must be *performing* competence while something underneath is broken. The feral thread must stay alive under the surface. Her perfect record isn't a sign of healing; it's a mask she's wearing so they don't look too closely at her pocket.

## 3. SCENE-BY-SCENE OUTLINE & FIXES

### Scene 1: Terin's Extraction (The Mask of Competence)
* **Current State:** Elara is perfectly professional, feeling only a twinge of "ash" when reciting the Archive mantra.
* **The Fix:** Frame her precision as a desperate performance. She is hyper-aware of the seed pod in her pocket.
* **The Specific Detail (The Arboretum Moment):** When she sees Terin's memory of the Arboretum, she currently just "leaves it untagged." This is too passive. Make it physical. Her fingers hover. The seed pod in her pocket warms in response to the memory on the screen. She actively, deliberately destroys a piece of a child's memory to protect her own secret. It is a feral, selfish act.

### Scene 2: Varnin's Extraction (The Interrogation)
* **Current State:** Varnin's dialogue is novelistic exposition ("Your extraction record is unparalleled... Even after your personal loss").
* **The Fix:** Make Varnin sharper, more oblique. He doesn't state her resume. He talks around the edges of her secret. He knows something is wrong with Mira's extraction.
* **Show, Don't Tell:** Remove "appropriate deference." Show Elara's deference through physical posture or voice modulation—and show how that posture cracks when he mentions Mira.

### Scene 3: The Reality Bleed (The Vision)
* **Current State:** The vision of alternate-Mira in the garden feels like a standard sci-fi hologram. Described by a narrator ("Eight minutes of fractured reality").
* **The Fix:** Ground it in physical sensation. The transition shouldn't be visual first; it should be sensory (smell of soil, sudden shift in gravity/temperature). Elara doesn't just "see" an older Mira; she feels the terrifying, impossible physical presence of her.
* **The Exit:** When she leaves, she doesn't just walk away. The seed pod is burning against her thigh. She is leaving to protect the pod, not just because she's disillusioned.

## 4. INVIOLABLE RULES FOR THIS DRAFT

1. **The Personality Preservation Rule:** Keep "The words tasted like ash." Keep Terin's question ("Will I dream in the Archives?"). Keep Elara's final defiance ("Let it flag"). These are load-bearing voice moments.
2. **The Feral Thread:** Elara is not a hero realizing the system is corrupt. She is an animal realizing the cage she's in might threaten the thing she's hiding.
3. **The Em-Dash Diet:** The current draft has 21 em-dashes. This is a hard ceiling. Target 10-12. Use them only for genuine cognitive interruption, not as a rhythmic crutch. Periods hit harder.
4. **No Narrator Intrusion:** Do not tell us "she maintained perfect compartmentalization." Show us her forcing her hands to stop trembling while adjusting a dial.

## 5. CONTINUITY / STAGING CHECK
* **The Seed Pod:** Must remain in her pocket, bare against the fabric, occasionally warming. (In Chapter 3, she will transfer it to a preservation case. We don't need to do that here, but we must establish that carrying it bare is becoming physically or emotionally unsustainable).
* **No Staging Errors:** The physical space of the extraction chamber must remain coherent. If she is touching a console, she cannot simultaneously be standing by the door.
