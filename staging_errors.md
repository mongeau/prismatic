# STAGING & PHYSICAL LOGIC ERRORS

## Chapter 1: "The Seed"
**ERROR 1 (Critical): Suspension gel vs. pillow.**
Line 5: "Inside, Mira floated in suspension gel." Line 22: "She reached under her pillow." A child floating in suspension gel cannot have a pillow. The gel and the pillow belong to two different physical environments.
**Fix:** Clarify that Mira is in a quarantine bed, not yet in suspension gel. The gel is part of the extraction process that comes later. The membrane is a quarantine barrier wall, not a gel membrane.

**ERROR 2 (Minor): Membrane physics.**
Line 8: "Mira's fingers trembled against the membrane between them." Line 26: "She pressed the seed against the transfer slot—a portal designed for medical samples." If there's a solid membrane between them, how does Mira pass the seed? The transfer slot resolves this, but the staging needs to be clearer — the membrane has a built-in transfer slot. Currently reads as two separate barriers.

**ERROR 3 (Minor): Suspension gel reappears at the end.**
Line 43: "A blue-tinged hand... resting motionless in suspension gel." If we fix the opening to put Mira in a bed, we need this line to show the gel has been introduced as part of the extraction prep that happened while Elara was in the room. Or cut the gel reference here too and just describe the hand resting motionless.

## Chapter 3: "The Diver"
**ERROR 4 (Moderate): Seed pod location inconsistency.**
Line 135: "carried against regulations every day since Mira's extraction" — the pod is in her pocket.
Line 153-155: "The Memoria seed sat in a small preservation case she'd salvaged from a medical transport... Elara did not touch it. Had not touched it since placing it in the case."
But in Chapter 1, she was clutching it bare-handed in her pocket, nails leaving crescents in her palm (line 47). When did she transfer it to a preservation case? And if she "had not touched it since placing it in the case," that contradicts the end of Chapter 2 where she's carrying it in her pocket (line 135).
**Fix:** Add a brief transitional beat — either at the end of Ch. 2 or the start of Ch. 3 — showing Elara placing the pod in the case. The "had not touched it" line needs to be reconciled with the pocket-clutching in Ch. 1-2.

**ERROR 5 (Minor): Pressure suit and inner pocket.**
Line 198-199: "her suit brushed against an exposed conduit... where the seed pod rested in an inner pocket." She's wearing a full pressure suit for a salvage dive. How does a seed pod in an "inner pocket" get exposed to an electrical discharge through layers of pressure fabric? The text acknowledges this (line 200: "Through layers of insulation and pressure fabric") but the electrical discharge triggering the pod's awakening through a sealed suit is physically unclear.
**Fix:** Either establish that the preservation case is mounted externally on the suit, or that the discharge was strong enough to penetrate the suit layers (which would also injure Elara).

## Chapter 8: "The Nexus"
**ERROR 7 (Moderate): Ship description contradicts escape velocity.**
The chapter opens with extended description of the Chronos Nexus's interior — corridors, systems, layout — while the crew is supposed to be in urgent escape mode. The pacing of the physical environment contradicts the emotional urgency. Characters stop to observe architecture while alarms are presumably active.
**Fix:** Strip the ship description to what characters perceive while running. No one catalogs corridor materials during an escape.

**ERROR 8 (Minor): Dimensional current entry — five consecutive description sentences.**
When the ship enters the dimensional current, there are five consecutive sentences of sensory description with zero character reaction interspersed. The physical experience of dimensional transit is described externally rather than through a character's body.
**Fix:** Interleave character physical reactions (nausea, disorientation, pain) with every description sentence.

## Chapter 9: "The Current"
**ERROR 9 (Minor): Seed pod location ambiguity.**
The pod is described as being in Elara's pocket in some scenes and "against her ribs" in others within the same chapter. If it's in a pocket, it's against her hip. If it's against her ribs, it's in an inner breast pocket or strapped to her chest. The physical location needs to be consistent.
**Fix:** Establish one canonical location — inner breast pocket, against the ribs — and maintain it.

## Chapter 10: "The Arrival"
**ERROR 10 (Moderate): Repeated description block.**
Lines 1037 and 1044 contain nearly identical descriptions: "Through the viewports, Aethoria spread before them in familiar silhouette but wrong in ways that grew more evident with each passing moment." and "At the far end of the platform, the humanoid figures they'd glimpsed during their approach remained motionless, watching with unnatural stillness—neither advancing nor retreating, simply observing with patient calculation." These sentences appear at the end of Chapter 9 AND the beginning of Chapter 10, verbatim.
**Fix:** Cut the duplicate from the opening of Chapter 10. The chapter should open with the docking sound, not repeat the previous chapter's final image.

**ERROR 11 (Minor): Airlock anticipation physics.**
Line 1066-1067: "The airlock cycled open at Elara's approach—not at her command, but in perfect synchronization with her intention. The inner door slid aside to reveal the outer door already opening." Then Pax says "Effect precedes cause by approximately 1.37 seconds." This is a deliberate worldbuilding detail (time running backward), but the physical staging of the airlock needs to be clearer — did the outer door open before the inner door? That's the disorienting detail that should be emphasized.
**Fix:** Clarify the sequence: outer door opens first, then inner door, reversing normal airlock protocol. Make the wrongness physical, not just stated.

## Chapter 12: "The Summons"
**ERROR 12 (Minor): Seed crystal location after integration.**
Line 1597: "The crystal wasn't in her pocket. Hadn't been since the integration." But line 1276 (Ch. 10) shows Elara removing the pod from her pocket and placing it on the pedestal. Line 1384 shows her removing it from her pocket again (after presumably retrieving it). Line 1387 shows Yukimoto taking it. Line 1459 shows it integrating with the navigation interface. The crystal's journey from pocket → pedestal → pocket → Yukimoto → navigation core needs one clean tracking line to avoid confusion.
**Fix:** Add a brief beat in Ch. 10 or 11 showing Elara retrieving the pod from the pedestal before the escape. Then in Ch. 11, show Yukimoto returning it to her after using it as a shield. Then in Ch. 12, the "hadn't been since the integration" line works.
