## THE SEED POD / SEED CRYSTAL

The single most important physical object in the novel. Its behavior must be rigorously consistent.

| Chapter | State | Behavior | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ch. 1 | Seed pod | Stolen from Arboretum by Mira. Hidden in sock. Pressed through quarantine transfer slot. | "Deep purple-black, surface matte but somehow bottomless." Pulsed once against Elara's fingers in the hallway. |
| Ch. 2–7 | Seed pod | Carried in Elara's pocket. Occasional warmth/pulsing. | Described as "cold weight" then "warm weight" depending on emotional state. |
| Ch. 8 | Seed pod | Burns against ribs on approach. Vibrates like tuning fork near ship. Synchronizes with data core and ship (three heartbeats). Post-current: settles to steady hum "like a child sleeping against your chest." | NOT integrated into nav core in this chapter. Still against Elara's skin at chapter end. Phantom warmth = active warmth here. |
| Ch. 9–10 | Seed crystal | Pulses in navigation core. Resonates with dimensional current. Generates counter-fields. | Vibrations travel through deck plates. Synchronizes with Elara's phantom warmth. |
| Ch. 11 | Seed crystal | Resonance intensifies at dimensional fold/nexus. Illuminates bridge. Manifests Mira-entity. | Three-point resonance circuit: crystal + Elara's phantom warmth + nexus core. |
| Ch. 12 | Seed crystal | Surface pulses with Mira's neural patterns. Flares as coordinates lock. | Cassius's neural interface glows colors matching the crystal. |

**RULES (never violate):**
- The seed pod/crystal does not grant wishes. It is a resonance amplifier — it responds to genuine consciousness patterns, not desire.
- Its "cost" is that it draws on Elara's own neural coherence. Extended use fragments her identity.
- It cannot create Mira. It can only locate/connect to where Mira's pattern persists.
- The phantom warmth against Elara's ribs is always present after Ch. 8. It intensifies near dimensional instability.

## DIMENSIONAL TRAVEL: RULES AND COSTS

**Established Rules (never violate):**

1. **The Flux Drive** propels the ship through the dimensional current. It is not teleportation — it is navigation through probability space.
2. **The dimensional current** is not empty space. It is the architecture of all possible realities. It has tides, eddies, and storms.
3. **Dimensional travel has a cost.** Each transit shifts memory, identity, and perception. The longer the exposure, the greater the drift.
4. **The nexus** is a natural convergence point where probability streams intersect without collapsing. It is not safe for prolonged human habitation.
5. **Consciousness coherence** is the primary danger. The nexus dissolves the boundaries between variant selves. Without an anchor, you fragment.
6. **Observation collapses superposition.** The crew's presence at the nexus accelerated its instability (Yukimoto: "Like measuring a particle and destroying its superposition").

**Costs Established Per Character:**
- **Elara:** Memory shifting, identity recalibrating. Some changes permanent after each jump. (Ch. 12)
- **Mez:** Phantom scar on left thigh from a reality they never lived. Nose and eye bleeding during nexus exposure. Consciousness nearly fragmented. (Ch. 11)
- **Yukimoto:** Synesthesia overwhelmed — "All the colors taste like screaming." Temporarily unable to translate mathematical perception. (Ch. 12)
- **Nyara:** Mathematical certainty destabilized. Equations "bleed at the edges" during transit. (Ch. 9)
- **Cassius:** Neural interface changes color, spreads past elbow. Not infection — connection to dimensional patterns. (Ch. 11–12)
- **Pax:** Voice becomes blended/composite during dimensional stress. Adopts crew speech patterns. (Established throughout)

## CHARACTER CONTINUITY

### Elara Voss
- **Established backstory:** 17 years at the Archives. 8,412 successful extractions. Performed Mira's extraction herself (indirectly — she left the room but set the protocol).
- **The lie she lives by:** "The Archives preserve." She knows it's false. She said it to Terin (Ch. 2) and it "tasted like ash."
- **What she cannot say aloud:** "I watched them take you apart." (Ch. 12 — she chokes on it.)
- **What only she and Mira know:** The night before extraction, she cried in the sanitation chamber with the water running. Mira knew. (Ch. 12 — this is the proof of authenticity.)
- **Physical tells:** Hands tremble when emotion breaks through. Presses palm flat against sternum when grief peaks (Ch. 1 — established).

### Cassius Reed
- **The buried wound:** Nari. Left behind on the Sontaris Platform when shuttles reached capacity. Told her he'd find another way. Didn't go back. Six years of constructed identity built on this lie. (Ch. 12 — first revealed)
- **Neural interface:** Implanted pre-story. Changes color under dimensional stress. Spreads up arm. Colors now match seed crystal.
- **The variant he saw:** "Administrator Reed" — formal Archive attire, practiced neutrality, hollow eyes. The man he would have become.
- **Voice degradation:** Practiced modulation fails under emotional pressure. "Administrator Reed" bleeds through as identity fractures.

### Mez
- **The buried wound:** Cael. Found unshielded Flux concentration together. Cael crossed the dimensional membrane. Mez stayed behind out of fear. Cael never returned. (Ch. 11 — first revealed)
- **The admission never spoken:** "I was too fucking scared to follow." (Ch. 11)
- **Physical tells:** Fingers tap erratic rhythms when anxious — anchoring behavior. Sardonic edge is the first defense to drop under genuine emotional pressure.
- **Post-nexus:** Phantom scar on left thigh. Knows all the variations of that moment happened. Carries the weight of every version of themselves.

### Yukimoto
- **Synesthesia rules:** He sees mathematics as color and sound. Equations have texture. Dimensional anomalies have scent. This is not metaphor — it is his literal perception.
- **Post-nexus:** Synesthesia overwhelmed. "Everything's wrong. All the colors taste like screaming." Temporary, but the recovery will change how he perceives.
- **The thing he won't say:** He finds the dimensional current beautiful. More beautiful than anything in baseline reality. This is a danger.

### Nyara
- **The mathematical fortress:** She processes emotion through equations. This is not coldness — it is her only language for feeling.
- **The crack:** "You think I want this? A reality where mathematics confirms consciousness survives death?" (Ch. 12) — first genuine emotional break.
- **Post-nexus:** Mathematical certainty compromised. She stabilized Mez by calculating against their skin. This cost her something.
